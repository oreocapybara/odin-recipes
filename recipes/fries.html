Fries

Description

These crispy homemade fries feature golden potatoes with a perfectly seasoned coating. They're a delicious, crunchy side dish that's perfect for any meal or as a satisfying snack. The combination of simple ingredients creates irresistible fries that everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Garlic Salt
  • Paprika
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Slice the potatoes and place them in cold water to prevent browning.
  2. Mix flour and seasonings, then gradually add water to create a batter.
  3. Drain and dry the potato slices, then coat them in the batter.
  4. Fry the battered potatoes until they become golden brown and crispy.
