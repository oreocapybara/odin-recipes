Lasagna

Description

This classic homemade lasagna features layers of tender pasta and rich, savory flavors. It's a hearty, comforting dish that's perfect for family dinners and special occasions. The combination of traditional elements creates a satisfying meal that everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Canned Tomatoes
  • Fresh Herbs
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Brown the ground meat in a skillet until crumbly, then add onion and cook until translucent. Mix in canned tomatoes, half the parsley, garlic, basil, 1.5 teaspoons salt, oregano, and sugar.
  2. Boil lasagna noodles in lightly salted water until al dente.
  3. Combine cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, remaining parsley, remaining salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Layer ingredients starting with sauce and ending with mozzarella cheese until lasagna is fully assembled.
  5. Cover with foil and bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes. Remove foil and continue baking until top is golden brown.
